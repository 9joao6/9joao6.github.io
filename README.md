## [Cassette Restoration Terminal](https://9joao6.itch.io/crt)
![Thumbnail](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzI0MDQ1ODEwLnBuZw==/315x250%23c/COASDj.png)

An eerie anomaly hunt game where the player hunts for audiovisual and paranormal oddities in old advertisement VHS tapes. Made with Godot

## [ANTONBLAST 64](https://9joao6.itch.io/antonblast-64)
![Thumbnail](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzE5NzM0OTA3LnBuZw==/315x250%23c/gyWahI.png)

[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=beiSE4vwUgU)

A faithful 3D remake of ANTONBLAST, featuring the first level, a custom endless gamemode, 2-player coop, and custom player palette support. Made with Godot

## [Pipe Stream](https://9joao6.itch.io/pipe-stream)
![Thumbnail](https://img.itch.zone/aW1nLzE0MjEyNjc3LnBuZw==/315x250%23c/sZnIfp.png)

A short VR experience where the player carves out an ice block through the physical use of a hammer and chisel, taking care not to destroy whole chunks out of the sculpture. Made with Godot

## [Scrap Kings](https://9joao6.itch.io/scrap-kings)
![Thumbnail](https://i.redd.it/crdusb2vjpl51.gif)

A fully-featured demake of Nuclear Throne playable on mobile devices. Made with PICO-8

## [Crystal Raves](https://9joao6.itch.io/crystal-raves)
![Thumbnail](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/Mj2pfPXCxO8/hqdefault.jpg?sqp=-oaymwEmCOADEOgC8quKqQMa8AEB-AH-BIAC4AOKAgwIABABGH8gIygpMA8=&rs=AOn4CLDXPqYrWfHBxA_ikC2ajANV35-oDg)
